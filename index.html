The term "word art" describes a category of text-based postmodernist art employed by several contemporary artists since the 1950s. A simple definition of text-based art might read: "art that includes words or phrases as its primary artistic component".

On Kawara (河原 温 Kawara On, December 24, 1932 – July 10, 2014) was a Japanese conceptual artist who lived in New York City from 1965. He took part in many solo and group exhibitions, including the Venice Biennale in 1976.
